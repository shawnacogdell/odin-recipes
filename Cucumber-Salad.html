Cucumber Salad

This German salad has been passed through generations. Compliments Ruladen and Spaetzle well.

Ingredients

  • 2 English cucumbers, sliced very thin
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 3 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 teaspoons vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed

Directions

  1. Mix cucumbers and salt in a bowl; let sit for 1 hour. Drain cucumber in a colander, squeezing as much excess liquid as you can with your hands.
  2. Combine cucumbers and green onions in a bowl.
  3. Stir sour cream, vinegar, oil, and dill together in a small bowl; drizzle over cucumber mixture and toss to coat.
